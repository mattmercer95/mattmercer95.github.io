Argentina


Argentina, officially the Argentine Republic, is a country in the southern half of South America. It shares the bulk of the Southern Cone with Chile to the west, and is also bordered by Bolivia and Paraguay to the north, Brazil to the northeast, Uruguay and the South Atlantic Ocean to the east, and the Drake Passage to the south.

National Anthem: "Himno Nacional Argentino"

Population 45,605,826
Area 2,780,400 km2
Official Language(s) Spanish
Capital City Buenos Aires
Largest City Buenos Aires
GDP (2021 Estimate) $1.033 trillion
Currency Argentine peso ($) (ARS)
